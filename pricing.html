Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our  customer support  team.

Can I try magicplan for free?

Absolutely! Create a free account (no credit card required) and get access to two projects on 1 mobile or tablet device, including all features. Try it at your own pace. There's no time limit. If you have questions about data privacy, please take a look at our Data Privacy & Information Security FAQs.
How do I set up a subscription for my whole team?

Start by creating an account. You can then purchase a subscription via the magicplan Cloud and add the number of seats you need (1 subscription = 1 seat = magicplan app can be used on 2 devices). You can add seats to your subscription as you go — no upfront commitment.

After your subscription is purchased, you will need to invite members to your workspace. Here’s how.  

If you’re looking to implement magicplan in a larger team, request a quote for our Enterprise solution, and our sales team will get everything set up for you.
How easily can I cancel my subscription? You can cancel a subscription at any time by following these steps. We'll stop charging you after the end of your billing cycle. All subscriptions auto-renew unless you have canceled your subscription (this doesn’t apply to enterprise customers paying with an invoice).
Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription at any time? You can upgrade a subscription at any time, giving you instant access to more features. Downgrading is possible at the end of your billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, as well as in-app purchases via Google Play and the Apple App Store (please note: you can only purchase one subscription seat with in-app purchases). You have the choice between a monthly or annual payment. As an Enterprise customer, you'll have access to invoicing payment.
Which devices will magicplan work on - will it work on my laptop?

magicplan works on all iOS devices running iOS 14 and above as well as all Android devices using ARCore or devices with a gyroscope. On a laptop or desktop computer, you'll have access to all of your projects and files on the magciplan Cloud. Creating and editing floor plans is only possible with the mobile app.
How many devices can I use with magicplan?

Each subscription seat allows you to use magicplan on two mobile or tablet devices. A computer is not included with the device limit. You can access the magicplan Cloud from any web browser on any device.
Can I use magicplan in offline mode? Yes, you can easily use magicplan in offline mode. Any changes to your project will be stored locally. Once you have internet access again, your changes will automatically synchronize to the magicplan Cloud.
How do I train my team to use magicplan?

We offer free e-learnings and webinars and have an extensive Help Center that will get your team going fast! If you’d like a personalized training, we offer paid options that can be discussed with our sales team. For customers on an "Enterprise" plan, we offer custom online and on-site training sessions tailored to your needs.
